Affiliate Agreement

This Affiliate Agreement (this “Agreement”) is made and entered into by and between Pedal, LLC a Delaware limited liability company (“Pedal”), and {{ gymName }} (“Affiliate”), as of this date You have view this on {{ date }} (the “Effective Date”). By executing this Agreement, the parties agree to all of the provisions hereof, including the terms and conditions set forth on the reverse side hereof, which are hereby incorporated herein by reference.

  1. Description. As further described herein, Pedal and Affiliate hereby agree that Pedal shall provide a service to its customers whereby such customers pay a set price per visit or class to Pedal in exchange for the right to attend and use Affiliate’s facilities (and those of any other of Pedal’s affiliates) and that Pedal shall compensate Affiliate for each use of Affiliate’s facilities by each Pedal customer.

  2. Affiliation. Affiliate shall admit each Pedal customer bearing an official Pedal identification card to Affiliate’s facilities and shall afford such customer the same access, rights, and privileges to use Affiliate’s facilities as Affiliate affords to its other customers. Affiliate shall record each instance of each Pedal customer visit using such means as may be identified by Pedal from time to time.

  3. Payment. By company check postmarked by the 15th day of each calendar month during the Term, Pedal shall pay to Affiliate the sum owed. The amount per visit is no less than 8% of the Affiliate's lowest monthly membership cost plus Pedal's city base rate and on-demand bonus.

  4. Term. This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for one month beginning on the Effective Date and shall automatically renew on a month-to-month basis unless terminated pursuant to this Agreement.
